SHORT Single-precision MFLOP/s

EVENTSET
FIXC1 ACTUAL_CPU_CLOCK
FIXC2 MAX_CPU_CLOCK
PMC0  RETIRED_INSTRUCTIONS
PMC1  CPU_CLOCKS_UNHALTED
PMC3  MERGE
PMC2  RETIRED_SSE_AVX_FLOPS_ALL_SP_SCALAR
PMC5  MERGE
PMC4  RETIRED_SSE_AVX_FLOPS_ALL_SP_PACKED

METRICS
Runtime (RDTSC) [s] time
Runtime unhalted [s]   FIXC1*inverseClock
Clock [MHz]  1.E-06*(FIXC1/FIXC2)/inverseClock
CPI   PMC1/PMC0
SP [MFLOP/s]   1.0E-06*(PMC2+PMC2)/time

LONG
Formulas:
CPI = CPU_CLOCKS_UNHALTED/RETIRED_INSTRUCTIONS
SP [MFLOP/s] = 1.0E-06*(RETIRED_SSE_AVX_FLOPS_ALL_SP_SCALAR+RETIRED_SSE_AVX_FLOPS_ALL_SP_PACKED)/time
-
Profiling group to measure the FLOP rate of single-precision operations.
The event might have a higher per-cycle increment than 15, so the MERGE event
is required.
