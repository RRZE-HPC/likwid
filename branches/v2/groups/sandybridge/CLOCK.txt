SHORT Clock of cores

EVENTSET
FIXC0 INSTR_RETIRED_ANY
FIXC1 CPU_CLK_UNHALTED_CORE
FIXC2 CPU_CLK_UNHALTED_REF

METRICS
Clock [MHz]  1.E-06*(FIXC1/FIXC2)/inverseClock
CPI  FIXC1/FIXC0

LONG
Formulas:
Clock [MHz]  1.E-06*(CPU_CLK_UNHALTED_CORE/CPU_CLK_UNHALTED_REF)/inverseClock
-
This group gives you the actual core frequency in a measured time period.
This is important because due Speedstep and Turbo mode the frequency can
vary significantly.
